Family, Fire, & Flavor

At Oak & Ember, family comes first — and so does flavor. We’re a family-owned restaurant passionate about serving authentic, slow-smoked BBQ made with time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients.

Whether you’re gathering with friends, celebrating with family, or simply craving real wood-fired barbecue, Oak & Ember is your home for bold smokehouse flavor and genuine comfort.

Come hungry. Leave happy. 🔥

The Smokehouse Craft

Our menu is a tribute to the art of the fire. We specialize in Prime Angus Brisket, dry-rubbed and smoked for fourteen hours. Our St. Louis Style Ribs are finished over an open oak flame for that signature char.

Beyond the meats, we take pride in our "Roots" — seasonal sides like our cast-iron cornbread and honey-glazed burnt end beans.

The Hearth & Hand

Led by Pitmasters who have spent years perfecting the balance of airflow and wood moisture, we treat every cut of meat with respect.

Our front-of-house family is dedicated to "High-Country Hospitality," making every interaction feel like a Sunday dinner at home.

"We don't use timers here. We cook by the color of the smoke and the feel of the meat."
The Oak & Ember Team
