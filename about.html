Our Heritage

The Heart of the Hearth

At Oak & Ember, family comes first — and so does flavor. We’re a family-owned restaurant passionate about serving authentic, slow-smoked BBQ made with time-honored techniques.

From tender brisket smoked low and slow to fall-off-the-bone ribs kissed by oak fire, every dish is crafted with care, tradition, and a whole lot of heart.

The Come Up

From a Backyard to the Big Leagues

Every great smoke starts with a single spark.

Before the brick-and-mortar and the neon sign, Oak & Ember was just a modified offset smoker in a suburban driveway. We spent years testing wood types, perfecting our "Ember Rub," and feeding neighbors until the line stretched around the block.

In 2021, we took a leap of faith, trading the driveway for our first commercial kitchen. We faced the heat, stayed true to our low-and-slow roots, and built this place one tray of brisket at a time.

Est. 2021 — Built on Grit & Smoke
